FAQs: Coconut Skincare in Edmonton

Will coconut oil clog pores?

It depends on skin type and how much is used. A few drops on damp skin usually sinks in well. For oily or breakout-prone areas, keep it light and spot-treat dry patches instead of the whole face.

Can coconut oil go under makeup?

Yes‚Äîuse less than you think. Press 1‚Äì2 drops into damp skin, let it settle for a minute, then apply sunscreen and foundation. The finish stays smooth without pilling.

Is this safe for sensitive skin?

Formulas avoid harsh fragrances and use short, readable INCI lists. If skin is reactive, patch test on the jawline or inner arm for 24 hours and build up slowly.

How do I use coconut oil for hair?

Work a pea-sized amount into mid-lengths and ends, then brush through. For an overnight mask, use a touch more and shampoo in the morning. Curly hair often prefers a little extra on the ends.
